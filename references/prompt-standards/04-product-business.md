# Product and Business Prompt Standards

Use for product ideas, startup thinking, strategy, prioritization, scope review, business decisions, market sizing, and opportunity analysis.

## Required Prompt Inputs

- Decision to support: build, prioritize, launch, cut scope, expand scope, choose segment, evaluate opportunity.
- Audience: founder, product team, leadership, investor, GTM, technical team.
- User/customer and pain.
- Current status quo or alternative.
- Constraints: time, team, budget, market, compliance, technical limits.
- Evidence available: data, interviews, user quotes, metrics, docs, assumptions.
- Desired output: questions, recommendation, memo, plan, experiment, roadmap, market size.

## Office-Hours Style Questions

For early ideas, rewrite prompts to clarify:

- who has the problem
- what they do today
- why the pain is urgent
- the narrowest useful wedge
- how success will be observed
- what future state this unlocks

## Strategy Review

Add:

- scope mode: expand, selectively expand, hold scope, reduce scope
- what "10-star" means for this product
- tradeoffs and risks
- what should be explicitly out of scope
- practical next action

## Business Analysis

Add:

- decision frame
- metric or outcome that matters
- comparison baseline
- segments or dimensions to inspect
- assumptions and uncertainty
- recommendation format

Rewrite shape:

```text
请帮我分析 [product/business question]，目标是支持 [decision]。
上下文：[users/problem/current status quo]
约束：[team/time/budget/technical/market]
证据：[data/docs/interviews/assumptions]
重点：[segments/options/tradeoffs]
输出：[recommendation/memo/experiment/roadmap]，明确下一步行动和不确定性。
```
